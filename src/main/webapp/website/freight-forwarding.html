Freight Forwarding

Global presence, local excellence

Xeon provides the full suite of freight forwarding and clearing services, with operations in over 120 offices in 40 countries offering the experience of a global network with vital in-country experience and knowledge.

We manage thousands of shipments every year from almost every major sea and airport in the world. Our employees are intimately familiar with all the local customs laws and regulations - saving you time and money, and ensuring your shipments reach their final destination safely and on-time.
