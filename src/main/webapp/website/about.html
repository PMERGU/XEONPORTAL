Xeon Holdings

Strength through partnerships

“Strength”, as we possess the experience, knowledge and ability to exceed our customers’ expectations and “partnerships”, because we partner beyond transactional value and integrate into our customers businesses, providing intrinsic sustainable value.

Xeon Group is multi-branded and categorised into two divisions, namely supply chain (logistics) management and industrial products (gas & welding) sale. Supply chain management includes freight forwarding & clearing, warehousing, transport & distribution, and advance information technology services. The industrial product division includes distributorships for gas & welding products.

Our key differentiator is our relevance as a South African Black owned and managed business, and is underpinned by our information technology infrastructure, national distribution infrastructure, economic contribution to black women and community empowerment, level one contributor supporting preferential procurement, and compliance to ISO standards.

Xeon Group actively pursues innovation, constantly devising new process solutions, accommodating the domestic markets most pressing needs. As a company, our essence is encapsulated in the single statement, “strength through partnerships”

Customers trust

Our Customers trust in us drives our commitment to deliver. As a multi-brand company with several operating divisions, each business division is built on the premise that both our customers and our success are dependent on our partnership. What’s more, we understand that efficient and effective relationships are built on trust. This understanding informs who we are today – a company that harbours an extraordinary commitment to our customers, who place their trust in our ability to deliver.

In a market dominated by conglomerate companies, Xeon defines itself in the personalized and tailored business solutions where every customer is chosen carefully for the sustainable value to the partnership and partnership network respectively. Innovation through our entire business processes translates into productivity improvement to our customers – achieving our promise of “strength through partnerships”.
